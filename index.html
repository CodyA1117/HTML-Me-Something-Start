Introduction

Hey there! This article is about all of the jobs that I have worked in my life so far!

Life Guard

This was my first job. You could catch me whipping up food in the snack shack, and saving lives!

Landscaper

This was hard work but rewarding to look back at what you have created. I worked 3 properties mainly over the summers when I was not in school. Here I was able to mow grass, weed eat, build retaining walls, clear stumps and brush, and create gardens.

Flower Delivery

I worked for a flower delivery company, and always delivered that customer service smile.

Janitor

During my first degree, I worked as a janitor to pay for my regular expenses, as well as pay down school debt. This job was not bad. I would listen to podcasts, and clean after class.

Pharmacy Technician

Worked as a pharmacy tech, packing pills. This job was not fun, and really boring, would not recommend it in my opinion.

Business to Business Sales

Did affiliate marketing, and also did business to business sales for a company. I would sell businesses on marketing packages to grow their businesses.

Debt Collector

Worst job ever, only did it for 2 weeks. High stress and makes you feel dirty.

House Man at a Hotel

Setting up hotel events, such as weddings, and parties. This job was not bad, and I could also be a bartender while working this job after the event was set up.

Chat Support Specialist for Gym Owners

Would help gym owners with their supplement marketing, and increasing sales at their gym. Also helped them with orders. This job was awesome, it was remote, paid well, but ended up getting laid off.

Amazon Warehouse

Worked at an Amazon warehouse, stacking boxes and building box walls. This job was physically demanding but kept me in shape.

Amazon Delivery Driver

This job was pretty fun, but required a lot of walking around. I would average 10+ miles a day walking on the job. But I was able to listen to podcasts all day so that was cool.

T-Mobile Sales and Customer Service

I started working for T-Mobile, and this is where I am at present day. I am also in school for computer science, and life is very busy juggling everything.

Software Developer

To be determined. I want to become a software developer because I can work on the computer all day, and build cool things that are useful in the world.
